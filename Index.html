THE ALLMAN BROTHERS BAND

The Allman Brothers Band is a band out the Southern United States famous for their classic and southern rock music. They began in 1969 and first experienced commercial success in 1971. The foundation for the band was the brothers, Greg and Duane. Greg being the orgainst and singer, and Duane being the lead guitar player. Duane suffered a tragic death in the early 1970's from a motorcycle crash, it knocked the band of course for a while but they continued to tour in some fashion with mayn different members, until the 2000's.

They used a mushroom as their logo for many years.

This is a video of the Allman Brothers Band performing Whipping Post live at the Fillmore East Theatre in 1970.

They were a six man band with one singer/organ player, two guitarists, two drummers, and a bass player. These six members were the original group members.

The Allman Brothers Have won many awards throughout the years, here is a list of awards won by the Band!

  1. Grammy awards for Best Rock instrumental (1996)
  2. Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award (2012)
  3. No. 52 on Rolling Stone's Greatest Artist of all Time
  4. No. 49 on Rolling STone's list of Greatest Album's of all Time for Live at the Fillmore East
